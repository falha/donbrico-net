FAQ

Does it work on job sites like LinkedIn, Indeed, Greenhouse?

Yes. It detects forms on any website, including major job boards and ATS systems.

Is my profile data private?

Completely. Your profile is stored in Chrome's local storage on your device. It is never sent to any server.

What is Gemini Nano?

Gemini Nano is Google's AI model built directly into Chrome (version 127+). It runs on your device, which means AI answers with zero data leaving your browser.

Do I need an API key?

No. The free version uses Gemini Nano which requires no key. If your device doesn't support it, you can optionally add your own key from OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, or OpenRouter.
